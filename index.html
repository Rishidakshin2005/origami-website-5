How to make Origami cat

Step1:-Fold and unfold the paper in half both ways. Step2:- Fold the top and bottom corners to the centre. Step3:- Fold the paper to the centre again along the dotted lines. Step4:-Unfold the previous two folds. Step5:- Fold the paper in half. Step6:-Fold the flap of paper over to the left, open it up and Squash Fold it down. Step7:- Fold and unfold the sides in and top down along the dotted lines. Step8:-Make a Petal Fold using the creases from the previous step. Step9:- Fold the top flap of paper down along the dotted line. Step 10:- Make two Pleat Folds on the corners to form the ears. Step11:- Make another Pleat Fold to form the mouth. Step12:-Fold and unfold the left side of the model down along the dotted line. Step13:-Make an Inside Reverse Fold using the crease from the previous step. Step14:-Make another Inside Reverse Fold along the dotted line to form the tail. Step 15:- Fold the left side of the model behind along the dotted line. Step 16:-Completed the cat
